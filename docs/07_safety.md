# Safety

This repo documents a hobby / experimental project. It is not a certified appliance, a commercial product, or a guarantee that the design is safe for replication.

If you build, copy, modify, or use anything from this project, you do so at your own risk and under your own responsibility. The builder of this repo cannot take responsibility for injury, fire, property damage, or other loss.

This project uses:
- mains voltage
- high current
- hot surfaces
- exposed metal structure near electrical parts

## Minimum safety expectations
- earth / ground all intended metal chassis parts
- check continuity from mains earth to grounded metal parts
- secure cables so they cannot move
- keep mains and low-voltage wiring organized
- use proper strain relief
- insulate exposed joints
- mount the SSR correctly
- verify the enclosure is safe before power-on
- do not leave the machine unattended

## Public repo recommendation
Treat the repo as documentation of an experimental build, not a certification claim.

Suggested wording:
> Build and use at your own risk. This is a hobby project, not a certified commercial appliance.

## Safer design ideas
If you ever make a more replication-focused version, add:
- proper cable gland
- independent thermal cutoff / fuse
- dedicated earth lug to chassis
- safer low-voltage PSU choice
- standard sensor mounting method
